Our Roots

We are Dekouvri ‚Äî a movement born from the intersection of cultural pride and mental wellness, dedicated to transforming how the Haitian diaspora approaches healing.

Mission

Dekouvri transforms the mental health landscape for the Haitian community by providing culturally resonant, Haitian Creole-first resources and dialogue. We bridge the gap between tradition and therapy, fostering a safe space for authentic storytelling and self-discovery. Our mission is to empower every individual to unearth their own path to healing, one conversation at a time.

Vision

A world where the Haitian diaspora thrives in mental and emotional wellness, free from the constraints of stigma, judgment, or shame. We envision a community where seeking help is celebrated as a strength, and where every individual is equipped to reclaim their narrative and their peace.
